Research on N-doped Carbon Nanosheets with Ultra-High Specific Surface Area (Applied Surface Science, 2021,562, 150114).

Aug 2018-May 2021

Research on Monovacancy Coupled Pyridinic N Site (ACS Sustainable Chemistry & Engineering, 2021, 9 (3), 1264-1271)

Feb 2018-Feb 2021

• Report a concept of monovacancy coupled pyridinic N (MV-c-PN) active site, which is designed and successfully fabricated by the pyrolysis of well-designed precursor.

• This tuning induces stronger adsorption of oxygen-containing intermediates on the MV-c-PN sites and alters the ORR kinetics pathway.

• The designed hierarchical porous nature of CNx nanostructure facilitates the penetration of electrolyte and the transportation of O2.

• The CNx nanomaterial with such MV-c-PN sites shows outstanding ORR activity in alkaline solution, surpassing most of the reported metal-free catalysts, with its intrinsic turnover frequency (TOF) 7.26 times higher than conventional pyridinic N.



Research on Three-Dimensional Nitrogen and Phosphorus Co-Doped Holey Graphene Foams for Zn–air Batteries (Nanoscale, 2019, 11 (36), 17010-17017)

May 2018-Jul 2019

• Designed and synthesized controllable 3D nitrogen and phosphorous co-doped holey graphene foams (N,P-HGFs) as a high-efficiency ORR catalyst through structural regulation and electronic engineering

• The obtained N,P-HGFs showed a high ORR activity (i.e., a half wave potential of 0.865 V), long-term stability and excellent methanol resistance, which is superior to 20% Pt/C

• The remarkable ORR performance of N,P co-doped graphene is mainly due to the graphite N–C–P structure, where an enhanced charge density and increased HOMO energy level were confirmed by both experimental results and theoretical DFT calculations



Research on Graphene Rich in Graphite Nitrogen as Electrocatalyst for Fuel Cell (New Journal of Chemistry, 2018, 42 (24), 19665-19670)

Sept 2017-Jul 2018

• Designed and synthesized N-doped graphene with various contents of pyridine N, pyrrole N and graphite N. The N-G we obtained has a higher half wave potential (0.833 V) than the commercial Pt/C catalyst

Research on Dual-nitrogen-source Engineered Fe–Nx Moieties (Journal of Materials Chemistry A, 2019, 7 (18), 11007-11015)

Dec 2017-Jan 2019

• Find the synergistic effect of dual nitrogen sources: while Phen would anchor the iron atom via strong hexadentate coordination, urea could insert into the space of the Fe–Phen complex due to its small size. The constructed three-dimensional spatial geometric structure would effectively anchor and separate the iron atom to form active Fe–Nx moieties.

• The optimized Fe–N–C-800 shows high ORR activity with a half-wave potential of 0.883 V and also displays outstanding stability and methanol tolerance.

• The primary Zn–air batteries based on Fe–N–C-800 display high specific capacity as well as desirable stability.
